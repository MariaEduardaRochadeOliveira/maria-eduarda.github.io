About me

Title

Hey! I am Ana Júlia Monteiro, born in Ribeirão Preto , Brazil. At 12 years old, I co-founded the school's first FIRST® robotics team. In the competition, we dressed as Scientists ‘Cangaçeiros’, who historically condemned the government’s oppression. It protested the xenophobia against my region, which considers its citizens unintelligent due to the historical educational deficit. That episode increased 15x STEM participation in my school, instigated several institutions to initiate robotics, and awakened my passion for leadership. It was the beginning of my story! Currently, I aspire to enhance the educational scenario of Brazil to support the rise of young leaders.
